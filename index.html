Zhichar — The National Building Registry

The Digital Backbone of Bhutan's Urban Infrastructure

Zhichar isn't just a database; it's a mission-critical GIS platform designed for the Department of Human Settlements. By centralizing national building data into one spatial source of truth, this project modernizes how the country manages its physical landscape.

GIS Platform Spatial Data Infrastructure Building Registry

Zhichar Data Collection App

From the Field to the Cloud in Real-Time

I developed this mobile and web ecosystem to empower surveyors across the country. It allows for high-precision data entry, instant validation, and seamless syncing—even in Bhutan's most remote terrain.
